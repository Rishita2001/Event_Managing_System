DUET

Duet singing is a musical composition for two performers in which each performer complements the other's performance. So grab your partner, refine your voice and get on the stage to dazzle everyone with the power of your melodies.
Event Type: Duet
Team Size: 02
The following genre are allowed in this competition: Hindi / Bollywood / Folk / Classical / Semi-classical.

There shall be only one round in this event, which shall be on submission mode. A video is to be submitted to the organizers.

Judging Criteria:
1. Sur and Taal / Rhythm & Pace
2. Voice Quality / Texture
3. Song Selection and Performance
4. Voice Modulations
5. Overall impact

BEAT BOXING

Beatboxing is a form of vocal percussion which primarily involves the art of producing drum beats, rhythm, and musical sounds using one's mouth, lips, tongue and voice. It may also involve singing, vocal imitation of turntablism, the simulation of horns, strings, and other musical instruments. Beatboxing today is connected with hip hop culture, being one of "the elements", although it is not limited to hip hop music.
Event Type: Solo
Team Size: 1

There shall be two rounds in this event:
Round 1: Elimination
Round 2: Finale

Judging Criteria:
1. Originality / matching the set limit by original performer
2. Word Play
3. Variations
4. Performance
5. Overall Impact
